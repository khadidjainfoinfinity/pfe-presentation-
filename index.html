05 · Recommendation System

Collaborative + Content-based.

A hybrid pipeline that blends both approaches — collaborative filtering finds neighbours, content-based filtering finds similar products, then a weighted score combines them.

Pipeline
01
Data Collection
Purchase history, browsing, profile & ratings.
02
Collaborative Filtering
Jaccard similarity over user-item interactions.
03
Content-Based Filtering
Category, allergen and health-profile matching.
04
Final Score
Scorefinal(p) = α = 0.7 · ScoreCF + (1 − α) · ScoreCB
05
Cold-Start Fix
Fall back to category popularity when history is sparse.

Key formulas

Jaccard Similarity
Sim(A, B) = |A ∩ B| / |A ∪ B|
Final score
α · CF + (1 − α) · CB,  α = 0.7
Cold-start fallback
Top-N by category popularity

08 · Perspectives

Future improvements.

Where CartyGo goes next — both on the model and on the hardware side.

Next · 01
Computer vision for automatic product detection.
Drop the manual barcode scan — let a camera in the cart recognize items as they go in, removing a step from the customer's journey.
Next · 02
Deep learning to refine recommendations.
Move from Jaccard similarity to a neural model that learns from real customer behaviour and improves recommendation accuracy over time.
Next · 03
Deployment on real hardware.
Raspberry Pi, RFID readers and embedded tablets — validate the full system in real operating conditions, confirming CartyGo's viability as a market-ready solution adapted to the Algerian context.
